The Product

A collaborator built on context.

Rao keeps a living memory of your research, runs scientific agents on top of it, and improves the tools you already use, without you needing to change your workflow.

The same agent everywhere you already work

Rao connects to where your project already lives and shows up in the tools you already use.

  • Access Rao by chat in VS Code and the command line. Coming soon: browser extension, desktop app
  • Connect to context across your local files, Notion, and Google Drive
  • Share context with Claude, Codex, and Cursor via MCP, so that they don't have to figure out how files are related to each other from scratch—saving tokens and increasing iteration speed

A living scientific knowledge base

Rao's agents are powered by a map of your project context that evolves as you do research, keeping track of how everything fits together. This means Rao can:

  • Connect disparate ideas across your whole project when ideating with you
  • Finds the exact line you need and figure to reference across hundreds of papers, scripts, and notes
  • Stay up to date on its own as your work changes

Proactive agents tailored to your research

Because Rao already understands your work, it can augment your research work proactively. This looks like:

  • A writing assistant that allows you to generate and surgically revise papers drawing from your results and notes
  • Proactive literature search, surfacing work relevant to what you're thinking about at the moment
  • Never have to read scientific package documentation again with our skill builder, which teaches Rao how to use packages specifically in the context of your work
